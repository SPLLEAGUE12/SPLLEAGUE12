Game Rules

  • Each team consists of 11 players.
  • The game is played in 20 overs per side.
  • Each team can bowl a maximum of 4 overs per player.
  • The team with the highest score wins the match.
  • Bowling team has to complete their overs within 75 minutes.
  • In case of a tie, a super over will decide the winner.
